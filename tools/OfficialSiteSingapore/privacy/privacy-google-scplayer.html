This Privacy Policy describes how STARCRYSTAL TECHNOLOGY PTE.LTD. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use SCPlayer on Android devices distributed through Google Play. SCPlayer is a local media player. The current version does not show ads, does not offer in-app purchases, does not require an account, and does not upload your media files or playback history to our servers.

Summary: Almost all data stays on your device. The app package includes pre-installed Google AdMob and Google Play Billing components for possible future monetization, but they are disabled in the current version — no ads are shown, no purchases are offered, and we do not use the Android Advertising ID (AAID) for advertising. The only information that may reach us is what you voluntarily include when you send a support email through the in-app feedback feature.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

SCPlayer stores the following locally in app-private storage and does not automatically sync it to our servers:

  • Folder bookmarks (Storage Access Framework tree URIs you authorize)
  • Playback progress and recent history
  • Playlists and favorites
  • Browse state and preferences (resume playback, playback speed, gestures, subtitle settings, etc.)

You can clear playback history and playlists in Settings, or delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

3. Media Library Access

SCPlayer does not scan your entire device storage. You choose folders through the Android Storage Access Framework (system folder picker). We only read media inside folders you explicitly grant access to, for listing and playback.

4. Optional Feedback

If you tap Feedback in Settings, your device may open an email client with a pre-filled message that can include app version, Unity runtime version, device model, operating system version, and screen resolution. You choose whether to send the email and what to write. We use this information only to respond to your request.

5. Advertising & Analytics

The current version of SCPlayer on Google Play does not display ads and does not use third-party analytics SDKs. We do not collect or use the Android Advertising ID (AAID) for advertising in the current version.

The app installation package includes Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads) SDK components that may be enabled in a future release. In the current version, advertising is turned off in app settings: the AdMob SDK is not initialized, no ad requests are made, and no ad content is shown. Your media library, playback history, and bookmarks are not used for ad targeting.

If we enable ads in a future version, we will update this policy and the Google Play Console Data safety form before release, and we will comply with applicable consent requirements (including UMP for users in the EEA/UK where required).

6. Android Permissions

SCPlayer uses the following permissions for playback and related features:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — media controls on the lock screen and in the notification shade during playback. You may deny this in system settings.
  • Foreground service (media playback) — continue audio/video playback in the background.
  • Wake lock — keep playback stable during background playback.
  • Internet / network state — general connectivity checks; the current version does not upload your library or playback data to our servers.

The installation package may also declare permissions required by bundled but currently disabled Google SDKs (for example, components related to Google Mobile Ads or Google Play Billing). In the current version these permissions are not used to show ads, deliver purchases, or collect the Advertising ID for advertising.

We do not request access to contacts, SMS, call logs, camera, microphone recording, or precise location.

7. Third-Party Services

In the current version, SCPlayer does not actively connect to third-party ad networks or cloud account backends.

  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads) — SDK components are included in the app package for possible future use. They are disabled in the current version: no ads are shown and the SDK is not initialized for ad delivery. When disabled, we do not use AdMob to collect or share your personal data for advertising.
  • Google Play Billing — billing components are included in the app package for possible future in-app purchases. They are disabled in the current version: no purchases are offered and no purchase data is collected.
  • Email feedback — handled by your chosen email app and provider when you choose to send a message.

If we enable AdMob or in-app purchases in a future release, we will update this section and our Google Play Data safety declarations before that release.

8. Data Retention & Security

Local app data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall SCPlayer. Support emails we receive are retained only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry and meet legal obligations.

9. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Clear playback history and playlists in the app Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all on-device SCPlayer data.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com to ask questions or request deletion of information you sent us in support correspondence.

Because SCPlayer has no online account, there is no server-side account profile to delete.

10. Children

SCPlayer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us to request removal if you believe a child has contacted us with personal data.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy and change the effective date above. Material changes may be communicated in-app or on the Google Play store listing.
