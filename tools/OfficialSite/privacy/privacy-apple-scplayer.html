This Privacy Policy describes how Xinglan Wujie Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use SCPlayer on iOS devices distributed through the Apple App Store. SCPlayer is a local media player. The current version does not show ads, does not require an account, and does not upload your media files or playback history to our servers.

Summary: Almost all data stays on your device. The only information that may reach us is what you voluntarily include when you send a support email through the in-app feedback feature.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

SCPlayer stores the following locally and does not automatically sync it to our servers:

  • Folder bookmarks (document-picker / security-scoped bookmarks you authorize)
  • Playback progress and recent history
  • Playlists and favorites
  • Browse state and preferences (resume playback, playback speed, gestures, subtitle settings, etc.)

You can clear playback history and playlists in Settings, or delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

3. Access to Your Media

3.1 Folders you choose (Files / document picker)

You grant access by selecting folders through the system document picker. SCPlayer reads media only within those authorized locations for listing and playback.

3.2 Apple Music / on-device media library (optional)

On iOS, SCPlayer may offer access to your on-device Music / media library (e.g. songs and videos synced to your device). If you use this feature, iOS will show a permission prompt explaining that the app needs access to browse and play local music and music videos. We do not upload your library contents to our servers.

4. Background Audio

SCPlayer uses the iOS audio background mode so playback can continue when the app is in the background or the screen is locked. This does not send your playback data to us.

5. Optional Feedback

If you tap Feedback in Settings, your device may open a mail client with a pre-filled message that can include app version, Unity runtime version, device model, operating system version, and screen resolution. You choose whether to send the email. We use this only to respond to your request.

6. Advertising & Analytics

The current version of SCPlayer on the App Store does not display ads and does not use the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework because we do not track you for advertising. We do not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in this version.

If a future version adds ads or analytics, we will update this policy and App Privacy labels before release.

7. iOS Permissions & System APIs

  • Media library — optional browsing of on-device music/video library (with system permission prompt).
  • Document picker — user-selected folders; no broad photo-library access required for folder browsing.
  • Background audio — continue playback when app is backgrounded.

We do not request camera, microphone recording, contacts, calendars, or location.

8. Third-Party Services

The current version does not use third-party ad networks, social login, or cloud account backends. Feedback email is handled by your mail app and provider (e.g. iCloud Mail).

9. Data Retention & Security

Local app data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall SCPlayer. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry.

10. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Clear playback history and playlists in the app Settings.
  • Revoke folder or media-library access in iOS Settings → SCPlayer.
  • Uninstall the app to remove on-device SCPlayer data.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com for privacy questions or deletion of correspondence you sent us.

SCPlayer has no online account; there is no server-side profile to delete.

11. Children

SCPlayer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. International Users

If you use SCPlayer outside our primary region, your voluntary support emails may be processed where we operate support.

13. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy and update the effective date. Material changes may be announced in-app or on the App Store listing.
