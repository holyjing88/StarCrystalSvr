This Privacy Policy describes how Xinglan Wujie Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use SCPlayer on Android devices distributed through Google Play. SCPlayer is a local media player. The current version does not show ads, does not require an account, and does not upload your media files or playback history to our servers.

Summary: Almost all data stays on your device. The only information that may reach us is what you voluntarily include when you send a support email through the in-app feedback feature.

Data is encrypted in transit when you use HTTPS email or other secure channels. We do not sell personal information.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

SCPlayer stores the following locally in app-private storage and does not automatically sync it to our servers:

  • Folder bookmarks (Storage Access Framework tree URIs you authorize)
  • Playback progress and recent history
  • Playlists and favorites
  • Browse state and preferences (resume playback, playback speed, gestures, subtitle settings, etc.)

You can clear playback history and playlists in Settings, or delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

3. Media Library Access

SCPlayer does not scan your entire device storage. You choose folders through the Android Storage Access Framework (system folder picker). We only read media inside folders you explicitly grant access to, for listing and playback.

4. Optional Feedback

If you tap Feedback in Settings, your device may open an email client with a pre-filled message that can include app version, Unity runtime version, device model, operating system version, and screen resolution. You choose whether to send the email and what to write. We use this information only to respond to your request.

5. Advertising & Analytics

The current version of SCPlayer on Google Play does not display ads and does not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not collect the Android Advertising ID (AAID) for advertising.

If a future version adds ads or analytics, we will update this policy and the Play Console Data safety form before release.

6. Android Permissions

SCPlayer may request or declare only the permissions needed for playback:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — media controls on the lock screen and in the notification shade during playback. You may deny this in system settings.
  • Foreground service (media playback) — continue audio/video playback in the background.
  • Wake lock — keep playback stable during background playback.
  • Internet / network state — general connectivity checks; the current version does not upload your library or playback data to our servers.

We do not request access to contacts, SMS, call logs, camera, microphone recording, or precise location.

7. Third-Party Services

The current version does not use third-party ad networks, social login, or cloud account backends. Email feedback is handled by your chosen email app and provider.

8. Data Retention & Security

Local app data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall SCPlayer. Support emails we receive are retained only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry and meet legal obligations.

9. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Clear playback history and playlists in the app Settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all on-device SCPlayer data.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com to ask questions or request deletion of information you sent us in support correspondence.

Because SCPlayer has no online account, there is no server-side account profile to delete.

10. Children

SCPlayer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us to request removal if you believe a child has contacted us with personal data.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy and change the effective date above. Material changes may be communicated in-app or on the Google Play store listing.
