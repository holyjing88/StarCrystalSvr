2. Information Stored on Your Device

Vampire Dormitory may store locally and does not automatically upload all of the following to our servers:

  • Game progress, resources, unlocked content, and preferences
  • Language and UI settings
  • Cached assets for faster loading
  • Tokens needed to resume optional online features

You can delete local data by uninstalling the app or clearing its data where iOS allows.

3. Information Collected Online

When you use network features, we or our SDK partners may collect:

  • Device model, OS version, app version, language, and network information
  • Gameplay and product analytics events
  • Account or platform identifiers if you sign in or use cloud save
  • IP address and coarse region
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA) only where permitted by iOS and your choices

4. Advertising & App Tracking Transparency

The app may show rewarded video ads and other ad formats through third-party ad SDKs. On iOS 14.5 and later, access to the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for cross-app tracking requires your permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt where applicable.

You can change tracking permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and manage ad personalization in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

5. Analytics & Third-Party Services

We may use analytics and platform SDKs to understand usage, improve stability, and measure features. These providers process information according to their privacy policies and our instructions. The app may connect to backend services for optional login, save sync, remote configuration, or content delivery.

6. iOS Permissions & Capabilities

Depending on the build, Vampire Dormitory may use:

  • Network access — download resources, configuration, ads, analytics, and optional cloud save.
  • Local storage — save gameplay progress and cached assets.

We do not require camera, microphone, contacts, calendars, or precise location for core gameplay.

7. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the game and online services
  • Sync optional account or save data
  • Deliver and measure advertisements
  • Diagnose crashes and prevent abuse
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

8. Data Sharing

We may share data with service providers (hosting, analytics, advertising, authentication) under contract, and when required by law. We do not sell personal information.

9. Data Retention & Security

On-device data remains until you delete the app. Server-side data is kept only as long as needed for the service or legal requirements. We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

10. Your Rights & Data Deletion
