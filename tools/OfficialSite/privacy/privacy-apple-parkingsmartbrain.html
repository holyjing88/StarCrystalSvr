2. Information Stored on Your Device

Parking Smart Brain may store locally and does not automatically upload all of the following to our servers:

  • Game progress, level unlock status, in-game resources, props, and preferences
  • Language and UI settings
  • Anonymous user identifier (UUID) for session continuity
  • Privacy consent flags and onboarding state
  • Cached assets for faster loading

You can delete local data by uninstalling the app or clearing its data where iOS allows.

3. Information Collected Online

When you use network features, we or our SDK partners may collect:

  • Device model, OS version, app version, language, screen size, and network information
  • Gameplay and product analytics events (e.g. level start/finish, ad views, store interactions)
  • Anonymous UUID and optional platform identifiers for save sync
  • IP address and coarse region or country
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA) only where permitted by iOS and your choices
  • Apple in-app purchase receipts and product identifiers for entitlement delivery

4. Advertising & App Tracking Transparency

The app may show rewarded video ads, interstitial ads, banner ads, and splash / open ads through third-party ad SDKs. On iOS 14.5 and later, access to the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for cross-app tracking requires your permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt where applicable.

You can change tracking permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and manage ad personalization in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. Rewarded ads are optional where the game offers them as a choice for in-game benefits.

5. In-App Purchases

Certain App Store builds may offer optional in-app purchases (such as removing ads, virtual currency, or premium items). Payments are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may receive transaction identifiers and product information to deliver purchased content. Availability may vary by region.

6. Analytics & Third-Party Services

We may use analytics, attribution, advertising, and backend SDKs, including but not limited to:

  • Adjust — attribution and campaign measurement
  • ThinkingData (数数科技) or similar product analytics — gameplay event analytics
  • Ad networks / mediation SDKs — ad delivery and measurement
  • Backend / BMS services — remote configuration, cloud save, and operational settings

These providers process information according to their privacy policies and our instructions. The app may connect to backend services for optional save sync, remote configuration, or content delivery.

7. iOS Permissions & Capabilities

Depending on the build, Parking Smart Brain may use:

  • Network access — download resources, configuration, ads, analytics, cloud save, and in-app purchases.
  • Local storage — save gameplay progress and cached assets.
  • Haptic feedback — optional vibration during gameplay (where enabled).

We do not require camera, microphone, contacts, calendars, photo library access, or precise location for core gameplay.

  • Process and restore in-app purchases
  • Deliver and measure advertisements

9. Data Sharing

We may share data with service providers (hosting, analytics, advertising, payment verification) under contract, with Apple for App Store purchases, and when required by law. We do not sell personal information.

10. Data Retention & Security

On-device data remains until you delete the app. Server-side data is kept only as long as needed for the service or legal requirements. We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

11. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Adjust Tracking and Apple Advertising settings on your device.
  • Manage subscriptions and purchase history through your Apple ID account settings.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com for privacy questions or deletion requests related to data you provided to us or optional cloud save services.

Depending on your region, you may have additional privacy rights. Contact us to exercise them.
