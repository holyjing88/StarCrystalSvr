2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information stored on your device

Vampire Dormitory may store the following locally in app-private storage:

  • Game progress, level progress, in-game resources, and settings
  • Language and display preferences
  • Cached game assets downloaded for faster loading
  • Session tokens or identifiers needed to resume online features

You can delete most local data by clearing app storage in Android Settings or uninstalling the app.

2.2 Information collected when you use online features

When you connect to the internet, the app or integrated SDKs may collect or transmit:

  • Device model, operating system version, app version, screen size, language, and network type
  • Gameplay events (e.g. level start/finish, ad interactions, feature usage) for analytics
  • Account or platform identifiers if you log in or use cloud save
  • IP address and general region inferred from network traffic
  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) for advertising and ad measurement, where declared in the manifest and enabled by ad SDKs

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core gameplay, save/load progress, and deliver updates or remote configuration
  • Show rewarded video ads and measure ad performance
  • Monitor stability, diagnose crashes, and improve game balance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to online services
  • Respond to support requests you send us
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising

Vampire Dormitory on Google Play may display rewarded video advertisements and other ad formats through third-party advertising SDKs integrated in the app. These partners may use the Android Advertising ID and device information to deliver, measure, and personalize ads according to their policies and your device settings.

You can limit ad personalization in Android Settings → Google → Ads, or reset your advertising ID. Rewarded ads are optional in contexts where the game offers them as a choice to obtain in-game benefits.

5. Analytics & Third-Party SDKs

The app may integrate analytics, platform distribution, and game-service SDKs (for example, components related to ad delivery, event statistics, or channel reporting). These SDKs may collect device and usage information as described in their own privacy policies. We require partners to use data only for authorized purposes such as providing services to us, analytics, and advertising.

6. Android Permissions

Vampire Dormitory may request or declare permissions including:

  • Internet / network state — download game resources, remote configuration, online save, ads, and analytics.
  • AD_ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) — used by advertising SDKs for ad delivery and measurement where applicable.

The app may allow cleartext (non-HTTPS) traffic for specific legacy or development endpoints in some builds; production Google Play builds should prefer HTTPS where available. We do not request access to contacts, SMS, call logs, camera, microphone, or precise GPS location.

7. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service providers — hosting, backend APIs, analytics, and advertising partners that process data on our behalf.
  • Legal and safety — when required by law or to protect users, our rights, or public safety.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protection.

We do not sell personal information.

8. Data Retention & Security

Local game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Server-side game or account data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with law, or resolve disputes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove on-device information.
  • Manage ad personalization and reset AAID in Android system settings.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com to ask about your data or request deletion of information we hold from support correspondence or optional account services.

Depending on your region, you may have additional rights (access, correction, deletion, objection). Contact us to exercise them.

10. Children

Vampire Dormitory is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. International Transfers

Our service providers may process information in countries other than where you live. We take steps designed to ensure appropriate safeguards when data is transferred internationally.
