This Privacy Policy describes how Xinglan Wujie Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use Parking Smart Brain (停车救公主 / Color Defense) on Android devices distributed through Google Play. The game is a single-player casual puzzle experience. Some features require an internet connection to load remote configuration, optional cloud save, analytics, in-app purchases, or to show advertisements.

Summary: We store much of your gameplay progress locally on your device. When you use online features, we may sync game data to our servers or service providers. The app may show rewarded video, interstitial, and banner advertisements and may use the Android Advertising ID (AAID) for ad delivery and measurement where permitted. Optional in-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information stored on your device

Parking Smart Brain may store the following locally in app-private storage:

  • Game progress, level unlock status, in-game resources, props, and settings
  • Language and display preferences
  • Anonymous user identifier (UUID) for session continuity
  • Privacy consent flags and tutorial / onboarding state
  • Cached game assets downloaded for faster loading

You can delete most local data by clearing app storage in Android Settings or uninstalling the app.

2.2 Information collected when you use online features

When you connect to the internet, the app or integrated SDKs may collect or transmit:

  • Device model, operating system version, app version, screen size, language, and network type
  • Gameplay events (e.g. level start/finish/win/lose, ad interactions, feature usage, store page views) for analytics
  • Anonymous UUID and, on Google Play builds, Google account / Play Games identifier when available for save sync
  • IP address and general region or country inferred from network traffic
  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) for advertising and ad measurement where enabled
  • Purchase receipts and product identifiers for in-app purchases verified through Google Play

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core gameplay, save/load progress, and deliver updates or remote configuration
  • Process optional in-app purchases and restore entitlements
  • Show rewarded video, interstitial, banner, and splash advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Monitor stability, diagnose crashes, and improve game balance and user experience
  • Determine regional availability of purchases, ads, and certain features
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to online services
  • Respond to support or feedback requests you send us
  • Comply with legal obligations, including health-gaming and age-appropriate content requirements in applicable regions

4. Advertising

Parking Smart Brain on Google Play may display rewarded video advertisements, interstitial ads, banner ads, and app open / splash ads through third-party advertising SDKs integrated in the app. These partners may use the Android Advertising ID and device information to deliver, measure, and personalize ads according to their policies and your device settings.

You can limit ad personalization in Android Settings → Google → Ads, or reset your advertising ID. Rewarded ads are optional in contexts where the game offers them as a choice to obtain in-game benefits (such as extra props, revives, or unlocked parking slots).

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and other regions where required, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) or equivalent consent mechanisms before serving personalized ads, where implemented in the app build.

5. In-App Purchases

Certain builds on Google Play may offer optional in-app purchases (such as removing ads, virtual currency packs, or premium items). Payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your full payment card number. We may receive purchase tokens, product IDs, and confirmation data to deliver purchased content and prevent fraud. Purchase availability may vary by country or region.

6. Analytics & Third-Party SDKs

The app may integrate analytics, attribution, advertising, and game-service SDKs, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play services / Google Mobile Ads — ads and billing-related services
  • Adjust — attribution and campaign measurement
  • ThinkingData (数数科技) or similar product analytics — gameplay and product event analytics
  • Backend / BMS services — remote configuration, cloud save, and operational settings hosted on our or our providers’ servers

These SDKs may collect device and usage information as described in their own privacy policies. We require partners to use data only for authorized purposes such as providing services to us, analytics, advertising, and fraud prevention.

7. Android Permissions

Parking Smart Brain may request or declare permissions including:

  • Internet / network state — download game resources, remote configuration, cloud save, ads, analytics, and in-app purchases.
  • Vibrate — optional haptic feedback during gameplay (where enabled).
  • AD_ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) — used by advertising SDKs for ad delivery and measurement where applicable.

We do not request access to contacts, SMS, call logs, camera, microphone recording, or precise GPS location.

8. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service providers — hosting, backend APIs, analytics, advertising, and payment verification partners that process data on our behalf.
  • Google — for Google Play Billing, ads, and Play services as applicable.
  • Legal and safety — when required by law or to protect users, our rights, or public safety.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protection.

We do not sell personal information.

9. Data Retention & Security

Local game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Server-side game or account data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with law, or resolve disputes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

  • Manage ad personalization and reset AAID in Android system settings.
  • Manage in-app purchase history through your Google Play account.
  • Open in-app privacy settings or tap the privacy button (where available) to view our privacy policy.
  • Email support@starlaneinfinite.com to ask about your data or request deletion of information we hold from support correspondence or optional cloud save services.

Depending on your region (including the EEA, UK, California, and others), you may have additional rights such as access, correction, deletion, objection, or portability. Contact us to exercise them.

11. Children

Parking Smart Brain is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Our service providers may process information in countries other than where you live, including China and the United States. We take steps designed to ensure appropriate safeguards when data is transferred internationally.
